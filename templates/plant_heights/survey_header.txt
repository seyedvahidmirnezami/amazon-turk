The goal of this survey is to measure height of plants from image by drawing lines. Please watch the following video for the drawing instruction <br />
<a href=https://www.dropbox.com/s/kqsqsunccqxyj80/Tutorial%20height%20measurement.mov?dl=0>Plant Height Survey</a> <br />
The pseudo transcript of the video can be found <a href=https://www.dropbox.com/s/ml80sknzhh8pqln/Plant%20Height%20Survey%20M1_SH.docx?dl=0>here</a><br />
<br />
Please maximize the browser window during this survey in order to provide accurate measurements.
Please do not change devices (i.e. switch to a phone or tablet) after you have clicked the next button below.

<script>
<!-- Record window size, monitor resolution, and DPI scaling in the "anno_metadata" embedded data. -->
Qualtrics.SurveyEngine.addOnPageSubmit(function (type) {
  var meta = {
    screenX: window.screen.availWidth,
    screenY: window.screen.availHeight,
    windowX: jQuery(window).width(),
    windowY: jQuery(window).height(),
    devicePixelRatio: window.devicePixelRatio,  // detect high DPI displays
  };
  Qualtrics.SurveyEngine.setEmbeddedData('anno_metadata', JSON.stringify(meta));
});
</script>
